01 · Framework Foundations
01 · 框架基础

Axis Definitions

坐标轴定义

X-Axis
X轴
Left ↔ Right
左翼 ↔ 右翼
↑

Measures the political content orientation of an artist's work — not their biography, market position, or institutional recognition. Left-wing practice critiques capital accumulation and speaks for marginalised communities. Right-wing practice embraces market logic or affirms existing hierarchies.

测量艺术家作品的政治内容倾向——而非其个人传记、市场地位或机构认可度。左翼实践批判资本积累、为边缘群体代言。右翼实践拥抱市场逻辑或肯定现有秩序。

v7.1: "Not engaging with political content" ≠ right-wing. Indifferent artists (■) are positioned by content orientation, not market presence.
v7.1校准：「不涉及政治内容」≠ 右翼。漠视性艺术家（■）的X轴由作品内容倾向决定，而非市场地位。
Y-Axis
Y轴
Authoritarian ↔ Liberated
集权 ↔ 自由
↓

Measures the relationship between the practice and power structures — not the political system of the artist's country. Authoritarian end: practice serves or is complicit with power. Liberated end: practice actively resists or escapes power structures.

测量创作实践与权力结构之间的关系——而非艺术家所在国家的政治体制。集权端：实践服务于或与权力共谋。自由端：实践主动抵抗或逃离权力结构。

Critical: A Chinese artist who paints domestic scenes is not "authoritarian" because China is. Y-axis is read from the practice, not the passport.
关键：一位画日常场景的中国艺术家，不因为中国是集权国家就处于「集权端」。Y轴从创作实践中读取，而非护照。
The Meta-Proposition
核心元命题
Geographic-Political vs. Artistic-Political
地理政治性 vs. 艺术政治性
↓

Geographic-political position: The political reality of the artist's country and cultural context — objective, external, unchosen. This is where history places the artist.

Artistic-political position: The actual relationship between their creative practice and power structures — subjective, chosen, enacted. This is where the artist places themselves through their work.

The distance and direction between these two coordinates is the framework's core signal — and the collector's primary area of interest.

地理政治坐标：艺术家所在国家与文化土壤的政治现实——客观的、外部的、不可选择的。这是历史放置艺术家的位置。

艺术政治坐标：其创作实践与权力结构之间的实际关系——主观的、可选择的、被实践的。这是艺术家通过作品将自己放置的位置。

两个坐标之间的距离和方向是框架的核心信号——也是收藏家最主要的关注领域。

Tension Views

张力视图

Horizontal = geographic-political pressure (open → closed). Vertical = artistic-political deviation (conformity → defiance). Node size = tension magnitude. Top-right = maximum tension zone.

横轴 = 地理政治压力（开放→封闭）。纵轴 = 艺术政治偏离度（顺从→对抗）。节点大小 = 张力幅度。右上角 = 最大张力区。

Left axis = geographic-political (authoritarian → open). Right axis = artistic-political (complicit → defiant). Chord curvature = tension — the more it bends, the greater the distance.

左轴 = 地理政治（集权→开放）。右轴 = 艺术政治（共谋→对抗）。弦的弯曲程度 = 张力——弯曲越大，两坐标系距离越远。

Derivation Process

推导过程

Selected exchanges from the research conversation that generated this framework — key inflection points where the logic shifted, deepened, or was corrected.

生成此框架的研究对话中的关键节点——框架逻辑发生转折、深化或被修正的时刻。

Phase 01 · Initial proposition — 初始命题
Collector
I need to build a classification framework for an independent collector. The collection focuses on the political stance of artists and their work. The foundational logic should be an X-axis (political left–right) and Y-axis (degree of governmental authoritarianism). On this basis, artists from different countries occupy different positions.
收藏家
需要制作一个独立收藏家的当代艺术品收藏分类框架，艺术收藏关注艺术家和其作品的政治立场和各类议题的讨论，所以希望这个框架的底层逻辑是一个X轴（政治左右翼）和Y轴（政府集权程度），在这个基础上，不同国家的艺术家处在不同的维度。
Framework origin
The initial formulation already contains a latent tension: "different countries" implies geographic-political positioning, while "political stance of artists and their work" implies artistic-political positioning. This distinction becomes the framework's central proposition.
框架起点
初始表述已隐含一个张力："不同国家"意味着地理政治定位，但"艺术家和其作品的政治立场"意味着艺术政治定位。这一区分将成为框架的核心命题。
Phase 02 · Transcultural themes — 跨国别议题
Collector
The discussion about labels is too limited. Maoist thought is actually a topic that foreign artists engage with — Mimmo Paladino studied Maoist-era Chinese visual culture and exhibited at the Forbidden City and the Great Wall. This is a topic capable of international dialogue, transcending national borders.
收藏家
上面关于标签的讨论有点局限，毛泽东思想其实是有国外艺术家在关注的，比如Mimmo曾经针对毛泽东主义的研究创作过一系列作品并在故宫和长城展览，这是一个可以国际对话的议题，超越国别。
Key correction
Issue labels recalibrated from "identity tags" to "dialogue structures." Political themes are not the property of any one nationality — they are shared, transcultural fields any artist can enter.
关键修正
议题标签从"身份标签"重新校准为"对话结构"。政治性议题不是任何国籍的专属——它们是共享的、跨文化的场域，任何艺术家都可以进入。
Phase 03 · Three-type system — 三类定位逻辑
Collector
Geography is still very important to this framework — if all artists converged there would be no need to classify. So the framework should still be based on geographic-political science. But for artists like Yan Ping, the "indifferent" political attribute is very important — can these artists be classified through the standard of art political science, so as not to forcibly distort the artist's subjectivity?
收藏家
地理政治学对于这个框架还是非常重要的，如果所有艺术家都趋同就没有必要分类了，所以目前还是基于地理政治学的框架为基础。但是例如闫平这类艺术家的"漠视"政治属性非常重要，对于这类艺术家是否能更偏重用艺术政治学的标准来分类，这样不强行篡改艺术家的主观呢？
Three-type logic established
Political (●) — Y-axis from practice. Indifferent (■) — Y-axis centred, color carries geographic context. Strategically ambiguous (▲) — between the two. The framework preserves geographic differentiation while refusing to let nationality determine political standing.
三类逻辑确立
政治性（●）——Y轴由实践决定。漠视性（■）——Y轴归中，颜色携带地理语境。策略性模糊（▲）——介于两者之间。框架保留地理差异，同时拒绝让国籍决定政治站位。
Phase 04 · The meta-proposition crystallises — 元命题形成
Collector
Through our discussion we have gradually discovered a meta-proposition worth serious attention: the definition of geographic-political science and artistic-political science, and the distance and tension between them. If we look at our analytical framework from this angle, do you think there is a better way to express it?
收藏家
我觉得随着我们的讨论逐渐发现了这位收藏家很值得关注的一个元命题，即：地理政治性与艺术政治性的定义以及两者之间的距离和张力，如果用这个视角来做我们的这个分析框架你觉得有更好的表现形式吗？
The framework's most significant moment
The distance between geographic-political and artistic-political positions is not a problem to be corrected — it is the framework's most generative subject. Three new visual forms proposed: dual-layer overlay, tension matrix, chord diagram. The collecting philosophy shifts: not "where does each artist stand?" but "what is the nature of the gap between where they are placed and where they choose to stand?"
框架最重要的智识时刻
地理政治与艺术政治坐标之间的距离不是一个需要纠正的问题——它是框架最具生成性的主题。三种新的视觉形式被提出：双层叠加、张力矩阵、弦图。收藏哲学转向：不是"每位艺术家站在哪里？"而是"他们被安置的位置与他们选择站立的位置之间的差距是什么性质的？"
Phase 05 · X-axis calibration — X轴校准
Collector
Why is Dadamaino right-wing?
收藏家
Dadamaino为什么是右翼？
Systemic correction — 15 artists recalibrated
The positioning of indifferent artists on the X-axis was using market position as a proxy for political content orientation. "Does not engage with political content" ≠ "right-wing." Sensory, spatial, and philosophical experimentation drifts mildly left as passive resistance to commodity logic. Only explicit market embrace or declared political conservatism justifies right-of-centre positioning.
系统性修正——15位艺术家重新校准
漠视性艺术家的X轴定位使用了市场地位作为政治内容倾向的替代变量。"不涉及政治内容"≠"右翼"。感知/空间/哲学实验轻微偏左（对商品逻辑的被动抵抗）。只有明确的市场拥抱或明确的政治保守主义立场才能为偏右定位提供依据。
