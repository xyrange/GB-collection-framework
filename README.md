# 地理政治性与艺术政治性的张力
## The Tension between Geopolitics and Artistic Politics

> *A private collector's research framework — version 7.1*

---

## Overview

This repository hosts a bilingual (EN/中) research framework for contemporary art collection, built around a single meta-proposition:

> **The distance and tension between an artist's geographic-political position and their artistic-political position is the most information-rich signal in the entire framework.**

The collector's vantage point is rooted in China. The framework demonstrates that political themes and stances are not confined within national borders — they are shared, dialogic, and transcultural.

## The Two Coordinate Systems

### Geographic-Political Position 地理政治坐标

The political reality of the artist's country and cultural context — **objective, external, unchosen**. This is where history places the artist.

### Artistic-Political Position 艺术政治坐标

The actual relationship between their creative practice and power structures — **subjective, chosen, enacted**. This is where the artist places themselves through their work.

The **distance and direction** between these two coordinates is the framework's core signal.

---

## Axis Definitions 坐标轴定义

### X-Axis: Left ↔ Right / 左翼 ↔ 右翼

Measures **political content orientation** of an artist's work — not biography, not market position.

| Left 左翼 | Right 右翼 |
|---|---|
| Critiques capital accumulation | Embraces market logic |
| Speaks for marginalised communities | Treats commodification as neutral or positive |
| Challenges existing power relations | Affirms existing hierarchies |

> **v7.1 calibration:** "Not engaging with political content" ≠ right-wing. Indifferent artists (■) are positioned by content orientation, not market presence.

### Y-Axis: Authoritarian ↔ Liberated / 集权 ↔ 自由

Measures the **relationship between practice and power structures** — not the political system of the artist's country.

> **Critical:** A Chinese artist who paints domestic scenes is not "authoritarian" because China is. Y-axis is read from the practice, not the passport.

## Three Artist Types 三类艺术家

| Symbol | Type | Logic |
|---|---|---|
| ● | Political 政治性 | Y-axis from practice's actual relationship to power. Geography is context, not determinant. |
| ■ | Indifferent 漠视性 | Y-axis near centre (±0.15). Practice outside complicity/defiance entirely. Color carries geographic context. |
| ▲ | Strategically Ambiguous 策略性模糊 | Political awareness present but deliberately withheld. Y-axis by degree of embedding in power systems. |
| ◆ | Collected 已收藏 | Pentagon marker — works in the collection. Larger node, persistent label. |

## Four Tension Patterns 四种张力模式

| Pattern | Tension | Description | Key examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rupture 撕裂** | ████████████ Max | Practice defines itself *against* an extreme external condition | Ai Weiwei, Yoan Capote |
| **Drift 漂移** | ███████░░░░░ Mod | Practice moves away from what the external system would predict | Liu Xiaodong, Xu Bing, Lee Bul |
| **Diaspora Paradox 离散悖论** | █████████░░░ Complex | Carries one system's memory while operating inside another's | Yan Pei-Ming, Danh Vo, Chen Zhen |
| **Coincidence 重合** | █░░░░░░░░░░░ Min | Practice reflects or accepts the external system | Lenz Geerk, Bruno Gadenne |
